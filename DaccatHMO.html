ABOUT US

Daccat Healthcare Management Organization is a pioneer healthcare management provider that specilizes in providing medical health insurance across board within Imo Sate and Beyond.

The stark reality off affordable healthcare being beyond the raech of the vast majority of the populace stares us in the face. For most people affordable healthcare has becoming a pipe-dream that may never materialize. A nightmare that hunts their every attempt to have a peaceful dream. This situation has forced most of our people to seek alternate options with it attendant risk. The situation is worse amongst low income earners and the disappearing middle class. We tend to stem this ugly tide by introducing affordable health insurance for the populace to stem the shock of exorbitant healthcare cost when the undesirable eventually happens.
